# Magic Living — Future Ideas

Ideas and features to revisit in future sessions. Not committed to — just captured so we don't forget.

---

## Compact/Detailed View Toggle
Bills page: add a toggle (like ADO's compact vs detailed view) in the header area. Compact mode shows just bill name + amount per row — super scannable across pay periods. Detailed mode shows the full current layout with due dates, badges, and actions. Persist the preference.

## Bill Categories
Add a `category` field to bills (e.g. Housing, Utilities, Subscriptions, Insurance, Transportation). This would enable:
- Category breakdown in the bills page hero (e.g. "Housing $1,850 · Subscriptions $89")
- Grouping bills by category as an alternative to paycheck timing
- Better spending insights when cross-referenced with transaction categories

## Activity Page
Move the recent activity feed to its own dedicated route rather than cluttering the home page. Could include filtering by type (bill paid, bill added, etc.) and date ranges.

## Spending Dashboard (Phase 2)
- `categorySettings` table for user-managed essential/non-essential tagging
- Headline stats: Total Spending | Essential | Non-Essential | % change vs last month
- Category breakdown with inline reclassification
- Drill-down: click category → see transactions
- Month-over-month comparison

## Heads Up Card
Conditional card on home page that only appears when something notable happens:
- Unusual charges (> 2x average for a merchant/category)
- CC balance trends (up or down significantly)
- Spending pace warnings ("on track to spend 30% more than last month")

## 7-Day Forecast
Combined view of upcoming bills + expected income for the next 7 days with a net cash flow number.

## Savings Rate
Show savings rate (savings / income) as a metric somewhere — maybe in the home hero or spending dashboard.
